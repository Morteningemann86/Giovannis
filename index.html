Our Philosophy

Quality

The best flavors come from the best ingredients. This is why we only use high quality milk, berries, chocolate etc. when preparing our ice-cream.

Craft

We have perfected our ability to create tasteful ice-cream that fills you with flavors. By striving towards perfection we constantly improve upon ourselves and the result is clear: Giovannis offers the best ice-cream in Hillerød

Taste

At Giovanni's we offer all the classical tastes such as vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry. We are also proud of our liqurice ice-cream, which goes really well with a scoop of caramel.
